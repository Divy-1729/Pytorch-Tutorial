# Pytorch-Tutorial

A tutorial for mastering PyTorch.

Pytorch is the most widely used library for building ML models. It also gives you much more flexibility than TensorFlow. 

This tutorial (or collection of notes) is my attempt at distilling from this awesome [resource](https://www.learnpytorch.io/00_pytorch_fundamentals/). I plan to write this and finish it within a week.

This tutorial does not go into the theoretical aspects of Machine Learning/Deep Learning/Neural Networks. I would recommend you seek an external source to obtain that knowledge. This tutorial is best suited for people who want to refresh their knowledge of Pytorch, or have been working with Tensorflow/JAX and want to pivot to Pytorch.

At the time of writing, I believe the first three chapters are sufficient to gain a foundation. Every chapter henceforth is relevant purely on your specific domain of choice.

If I have the time, I may include another chapter for NLP.

This repo is organized by chapter numbers in the original resource. Each chapter contains notes in a Jupyter notebook and exercises generated by GPT and curated by me. 

Future plan is to deploy this to the web.

Each chapter has a recommended Spotify Playlist to go with.

Good Luck!
